Let's Fight Icing

What is Icing? You may have read in the news recently about the owners of Internal Combustion Engines (ICE) using their vehicles as tools to prevent Tesla and other Electric Vehicle owners from accessing charging stations, while threatening and intimidating these users. The anger that ICE owners are expressing is misplaced and wrong. To combat this trend and to bring visibility to this issue we encourage EV owners and the general public to post photos and details when they witness icing.
